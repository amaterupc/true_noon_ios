## 2. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

### 2.1 Location Information

The App uses your device's location to accurately calculate true solar time, sunrise, and sunset times. Location information is used solely for the following purposes:

| Type of Information | Purpose | Storage Location |
|---------------------|---------|------------------|
| Latitude & Longitude | Solar position calculation, sunrise/sunset time computation | Device only |

**Important:** Location information is processed only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

### 2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

- Personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number
- Payment or credit card information
- Contacts, photos, or other data stored on your device
- Usage history or in-app behavioral data

---

## 3. How We Use Information

The location information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

1. **True Solar Time Calculation:** To display accurate solar time for your current location
2. **Equation of Time Calculation:** To compute the difference between standard time and solar time
3. **Sunrise/Sunset Display:** To provide accurate sunrise and sunset times for your location

---

## 4. Information Sharing

The App does not share any collected information with third parties. All data, including location information, is processed solely on your device.

---

## 5. Data Storage

The App does not store location information on any server. All calculations are performed in real-time on your device, and location information is used only temporarily.

Settings information (such as manually entered latitude/longitude) is stored in your device's local storage, but this information is never transmitted externally.

## 6. Security
